Welcome to the Duke OLLI Digital Explorers SIG website. The current content on this site is primarily for OLLI folks who participated in the Fall 2025 "Tech Skills 101" course and the SIG sessions we are planning for the OLLI Sprint 2026 term.

If you are an OLLI member who participated in the Fall 2025 "Tech Skills 101" course, I have shared the URL to this site with you so that you can act as a reviewer of the draft content and contribute ideas for the SIG meetings we plan/schedule for the Spring 2026 term. At this stage in "setting up" this site, my focus with the content on this site is planning the initial set of meetings that will be scheduled for the Spring 2026 term.

First, recall we discussed finding some free VPN service that we could use in our SIG sessions. I have found such a VPN services and added a page and some "how to" guides to help you set it up on your device(s). Please review those and attempt to set up your device(s) before our first session on January 23.

Second, I have come up with topics/themes for our first three sessions; check out the schedule page to see them. Feel free to email me with any feedback you have on them.
